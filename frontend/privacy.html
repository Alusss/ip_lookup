Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Data Collection

IP Lookup does not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII).

  • IP Addresses: Your IP address is used solely to display the lookup result. It is not logged, stored, or persisted in any database.
  • Request Logs: Operational logs contain masked IP addresses (e.g., 203.0.113.0 for IPv4) and are retained for a maximum of 30 days for security monitoring purposes only.
  • No Cookies: This site does not use cookies, localStorage, or any client-side tracking technology.
  • No Analytics Cookies: We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first analytics tool that does not use cookies or collect PII.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. API responses are sent directly to your browser and are not cached or recorded.

Third-Party Services

This service uses Cloudflare as CDN and DNS provider. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to their edge network processing. We do not control what data Cloudflare collects at their edge.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access your data (we hold none)
  • Request deletion of your data (nothing to delete)
  • Object to processing (no processing occurs)

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
